# Structly: Life Optimization Platform

> **Note:** This repository contains the **Prototype** version of Structly. The current stable code is located in the `master` branch. Built using **Google AI Studio**.

## 🧬 What is Structly?

Structly is not just another productivity app; it is a life optimization platform designed to help users understand and improve the underlying structure of their lives. Instead of focusing on surface-level metrics like "tasks completed," Structly identifies hidden patterns, dependencies, and leverage points that drive real-world outcomes.

By mapping the intersections of habits, decisions, environments, and goals, Structly shifts the approach from "doing more" to **"doing what actually matters."** It combines systems thinking, behavioral insights, and intuitive visualization to help you diagnose root problems and design a more intentional life.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎯 Core Goal Management
* **Strategic Decomposition:** Transform long-term visions into actionable short-term milestones automatically.
* **Milestone Progress Bars:** Visual indicators that provide "The Big Picture" without the clutter.
* **Smart Consistency Tracking:** A dedicated focus on momentum and streaks rather than just binary completion.
* **Adaptive Persistence:** Tasks move with you. If a task is skipped 3 times, the **"Task Debt" check** triggers to help you delete, delegate, or redefine it.
* **Identity-Based Mode:** Toggle between specialized UI/UX modes like *“Student Exam Mode,”* *“Founder Sprint Mode,”* or *“Recovery Mode.”*

### 🛠️ Smart Daily Operations
* **The "Navigator" Daily Planning:** A low-stress merge of your pending work and real-time calendar availability.
* **Energy-Based Tasking:** Tag tasks by energy level (High/Deep Work vs. Low/Admin). Structly suggests the right task for your current "vibe."
* **The "Starting Block" Prep:** AI-generated drafts, outlines, or resource links for every task so you never face a blank page.
* **Cognitive Load Meter:** Real-time monitoring of your daily capacity. If you're over-extended, Structly will suggest reducing your workload.

### 🤖 Advanced AI Support (The "Chief of Staff" Layer)
* **Agentic Task Automation:** AI agents that don't just remind you—they do the work (researching, drafting emails, booking appointments).
* **Context Stitching:** Relevant data (notes, emails, docs) is automatically pulled into the task view. No more hunting for info.
* **AI Voice Journaling:** An end-of-day audio check-in. Vent or summarize your day, and Structly automatically updates your Plan of Action (POA) for tomorrow.
* **Next-Action AI Summaries:** Ultra-brief overviews focusing solely on the very next needle-moving step.

### 📈 Frictionless Insights
* **Zero-Effort Time Allocation:** Automated tracking via integrations to show where your time *actually* goes (no manual pie charts needed).
* **The "Clean Slate" Protocol:** An automated end-of-week reset that clears low-priority "clutter" tasks to prevent "To-Do List debt."
* **Decision Simulator:** "If you do X today, here’s how your week changes."
